Target Audience

The target audience for this website is any person that has to go out of their home, be it to go to work, go to school, shopping, hike, ride a bike, or wathever other reason. Whatever reason someone goes out, is always a good idea to know what the weather is going to be later in the day. Also, thanks to the extended forecast, the audience for this website is anyone who likes to plan their activities ahead.

Personas

Charles (32) is a young father of two. He and his wife enjoy spendig time with their kids playing outside. He works in an office downtown, so he leaves early in the morning and get back home in the afternoon. Charles loves his classic car, so much that he doesn't like to drive it under bad weather.

Marlene (20) is taking a sabbatical year before returning to school. She loves everything about keeping her body healthy, so she loves to run, ride bikes, and hike. She posts everyday in her social media accounts pictures and videos of her adventures. Also, she loves to go out with friends. Her favorite is Thai food.

Scenarios

* Charles wants to cook a barbecue for her family. He buys everything he needs a couple days before, but that morning sees some suspicious clouds on the horizon. The forecast says there's a 50/50 chance of rain, but he can use the radar information in Weather GO! to know where the rain clouds are in real time. Finally, he is able to finish cooking 15 minutes before starts to rain.

* Marlene is planning to upload a new video to her accounts demostrating a new routine she learned from a exercises "guru". As the routine references the conection between her body and mother earth, she needs to shoot outside. She will use Weather GO! to plan which day she'll shoot, taking in account the time it will take filming.

* Charles is preparing a surprise for her wife for their aniversary. He's planning a romantic picnic, just like the one they had short before they first met. But, he needs to double check the weather to make sure he can do it outside. If the forecast says it will rain, he'll have to think in something else.
