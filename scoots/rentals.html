Rentals Information

Whether you want to ride trough the city, live a wild adventure, or travel the less known places of Cozumel, we have you covered

Our fine selection of vehicles, ranging from easy to handle scooters to all-terrain jeeps will suit any need you have.

All our vehicle options have liability insurance free of charge and unlimited mileage, so you can relax and enjoy

For your convenience we can deliver to various locations for any 2+ day rental. Then you can return the vehicle in any of our available locations.

Conditions:

A $50 security deposit is required to confirm reservations.
A valid drivers license is required in hand at time of rental.
No one under 12 years of age are allowed to ride the scooters.
Fuel charge not included.
Reservations rate requires 24 hour prior notification.
Payment via Google Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or Paypal.
